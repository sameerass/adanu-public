Do you live in Ghana? Want to give to Adanu in Ghana directly? Now you can!

Giving in Ghana

Adanu is a Ghanaian-led organization that needs support of Ghanaians – like YOU!

Whether you can give 1 cedi, 10 cedi, 100 cedi, or 1000 cedi, we are grateful for your contribution.

Adanu will use your donation to help fund a project in the Volta Region.

Please send donation to:
024 744 2600

Please note that rules for this type of donation are regulated by Ghana law, and not eligible for tax deduction in the United States.
